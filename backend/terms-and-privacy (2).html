AgroDoc AI is available to all Nigerian farmers, agro-dealers, veterinary extension workers, agricultural researchers, and anyone involved in Nigerian agriculture. The service is free for individual farmers. By using AgroDoc AI you confirm you are at least 18 years of age.

4. Privacy Policy — Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019

AgroDoc AI is committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019, administered by the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA).

4.1 What Data We Collect

  • Farm photos — uploaded for diagnosis (analysed by AI, NOT permanently stored on our servers)
  • Symptom descriptions — text you type describing crop or animal symptoms
  • GPS location — used ONLY to find nearby agro-input shops (not stored permanently)
  • Anonymous session identifier — a random token to track your usage without identifying you
  • Diagnosis feedback — your response to "Did this treatment work?" (stored anonymously)
  • Outbreak reports — anonymised disease location data you choose to report

4.2 What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, phone number, or email address (unless you contact us directly)
  • Bank account or financial information
  • Government identity documents
  • Personal health information about humans

4.3 How We Use Your Data

  • To provide crop and livestock disease diagnosis through the Claude AI (Anthropic)
  • To improve the accuracy of AgroDoc AI using anonymised feedback
  • To build anonymised disease outbreak maps for food security monitoring
  • To find nearby agro-input stores using your GPS location

📊 Anonymised and aggregated disease outbreak data may be shared with IITA (International Institute of Tropical Agriculture), FAO Nigeria, and the Federal Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development (FMARD) for food security research. No personally identifiable information is shared.

4.4 Data Retention

  • Farm photos — not stored permanently; processed and discarded immediately after diagnosis
  • Session data — automatically deleted after 30 days of inactivity
  • Anonymous feedback — stored indefinitely to improve AI accuracy
  • Anonymised outbreak reports — stored indefinitely for food security monitoring

4.5 Your Rights Under NDPR 2019

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your session data at any time
  • Request information on what data we hold about you
  • Opt out of anonymised data sharing with research institutions
  • Lodge a complaint with NITDA if you believe your data rights have been violated
