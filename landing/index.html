How It Works

Diagnosis in 4 Simple Steps

No training needed. No app to download. Just open and diagnose.

📷
1
Take a Photo
Snap a photo of the sick crop or animal directly in the app. Or describe what you see in words.
🌐
2
Choose Your Language
Select English, Pidgin, Hausa, Yoruba or Igbo. The diagnosis comes back in your language.
🔬
3
Get Your Diagnosis
AI analyses your photo and gives you the disease name, severity, and exactly what is happening.
💊
4
Follow the Treatment
Step-by-step treatment plan with Nigerian product names, mixing rates, and where to buy them.
