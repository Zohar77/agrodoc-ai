What We Do

Everything Your Farm Needs

One platform covering every challenge a Nigerian farmer faces — from disease to weather to market prices.

🌿
Crop Diagnosis
Cassava, maize, tomato, rice, yam, pepper, groundnut and more. Photo or text — full diagnosis with treatment steps and exact product names.
🐔
Poultry Health
Newcastle, Gumboro, Coccidiosis, Fowl Pox and more. Specific vaccine schedules, antibiotic dosages, and biosecurity advice.
🐟
Fish & Aquaculture
Columnaris, Gill Disease, Aeromonas, White Spot and more. Water quality guidance and treatment protocols for catfish and tilapia.
🐄
Livestock Care
PPR, FMD, Trypanosomiasis, Lumpy Skin and more. Covers cattle, goats, sheep and pigs with notifiable disease reporting guidance.
🌤
Weather & Planting
7-day weather forecast using your GPS location. Planting calendar for 7 major Nigerian crops with optimal timing advice.
💰
Market Prices
Live commodity prices from major Nigerian markets. Compare input prices across agro-dealers before you buy. Save money every season.
📒
Farm Journal
Keep records of every activity — planting, spraying, harvesting, sales. Your farm history becomes your credit score for lenders.
📍
Find Vets & Dealers
GPS-powered search for nearest agro-input shops. Searchable directory of certified vets and extension workers across Nigeria.
🗺
Outbreak Map
Real-time disease outbreak reports from farmers across Nigeria. Know what is hitting farms near you before it reaches yours.
